Pan-seer Steak

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add olive oil in the pan
  2. Add butter
  3. Add steak and fry each side for about 5 mins
  4. Add parsley
  5. Eat with anything you like, rice
