Stir-fried Chicken

Stir-fried Chicken called KyatSat, Burmese Cuisine

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Dried chilli
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Paprika
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Fry onions, garlic and ginger
  2. Dince and fry the chicken breast
  3. Fry dried chilli
  4. Add paprika
  5. Final step, add parsley and eat with rice
